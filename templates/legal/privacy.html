Privacy Policy

1. Information we collect

When you create an SPC Campus account we collect your name, username, email address, date of birth, and (optionally) a profile photo. As you learn, we store your course enrolments, answers, mastery progress, and payment records.

2. How we use it

Your information powers your personalised learning experience — tracking mastery, unlocking content, issuing certificates, and processing payments. We use your date of birth to confirm eligibility and tailor content.

3. Payments

Payments are processed securely by Paystack. We do not store your card details on our servers.

4. Data protection

We apply industry-standard safeguards to protect your data and never sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Your rights

You may access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting our support team.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at privacy@spccampus.com.
